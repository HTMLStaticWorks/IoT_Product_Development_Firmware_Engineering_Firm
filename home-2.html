Frequently Asked Questions

Do you help with FCC certification?

Yes, we manage the pre-scan and formal certification process with our partner labs to ensure compliance.

What RTOS do you specialize in?

We commonly build on FreeRTOS, Zephyr, and Mbed, depending on the silicon and project requirements.

Can you take over an existing failing project?

Absolutely. Our rescue teams specialize in auditing existing architecture, refactoring bad firmware, and redesigning PCBs to fix critical flaws.
