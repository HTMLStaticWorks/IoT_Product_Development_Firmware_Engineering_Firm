Product Development Lifecycle

End-to-end engineering from concept to mass production.

1. Architecture & Design

Feasibility studies, system architecture, component selection, and rapid conceptualization for IoT devices.

2. Prototyping

Custom PCB layout, 3D printed enclosures, and initial firmware to validate core functionality quickly.

3. Embedded Firmware

Robust RTOS or bare-metal C/C++ firmware development, optimizing for low power and wireless connectivity.

4. Manufacturing Support

DFM, certification support (FCC/CE), testing jigs, and supply chain consultation for scaling production.

5. Cloud Integration

Connecting devices securely to scalable cloud infrastructure for OTA updates and data telemetry.

6. Fleet Management

Continuous security patching, device monitoring, and mass lifecycle management support.
