About Us

We are a team of passionate hardware hackers,
firmware developers, and system architects.

Our Mission

Since our founding in 2018, IoT Engine has been dedicated to bridging the gap between hardware limitations and software potential. We believe that robust embedded systems are the foundation of the connected future.

Our goal is to provide enterprise-grade engineering solutions that reduce time-to-market while guaranteeing maximum security and reliability for deployments across the globe.
